# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and kind
- Refrain from any discriminatory or offensive behavior
- Consider accessibility and inclusive practices

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at [your email/contact].

## Scope
Applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and community channels.
